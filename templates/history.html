Our Sacred History

Infant Jesus Church

Kallettumkara, Thrissur

A Journey of Faith

Infant Jesus Church, Kallettumkara, stands as a spiritual beacon in the Diocese of Irinjalakuda. For decades, our parish has been a home for the faithful, nurturing a community built on the teachings of Christ and the traditions of the Syro-Malabar Church.

The history of our parish is a testament to the dedication of our ancestors and the guiding light of the Holy Spirit. From its humble beginnings to becoming a vibrant centre of liturgical and social life, the church has remained steadfast in its mission.
