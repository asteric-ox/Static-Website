Our Parish

St. Sebastian's Major Archiepiscopal Shrine at Thazhekad is a vibrant and historic parish of the Syro-Malabar Catholic Church, under the Archdiocese of Ernakulam-Angamaly. With roots tracing back to approximately AD 800, we are one of the oldest Christian communities in Kerala.

Our parish is home to over 1,200 families who come together in faith, worship, and service. Through our various parish organisations — including the Parish Council, Mathrusangham (Mothers' Association), KCYM (Youth Movement), and Catechism classes — we strive to build a strong, faith-filled community.

As a Major Archiepiscopal Shrine, we welcome pilgrims and visitors from across the Archdiocese and beyond, especially during our annual parish feast of St. Sebastian on January 20th.
